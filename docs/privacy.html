Privacy Policy — APC Dashboard Feed Last updated: July 21, 2026 APC Dashboard Feed ("the Application") is an internal-use software integration operated by American Patio Covers ("we," "us"). It connects to QuickBooks Online, via Intuit's API, solely to retrieve our own companies' accounting data for internal business reporting. Who uses this Application The Application is not offered to the public. It is used exclusively by American Patio Covers and its affiliated company to access their own QuickBooks Online data. There are no third-party users, customers, or subscribers of the Application. What data the Application accesses With authorization granted by our own administrators, the Application reads accounting data from our QuickBooks Online companies, including company information, invoices, and financial report summaries. The Application has read-only intent: it does not create, modify, or delete records in QuickBooks Online. How data is used and stored Retrieved data is used solely to produce internal business dashboards and reports. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections and stored within our own Microsoft 365 / SharePoint environment, which is access-controlled to authorized personnel of our organization. What we do not do We do not sell, rent, or share retrieved data with third parties. We do not use the data for advertising or marketing. We do not collect personal information from members of the public. Data retention and deletion Reporting extracts are retained only as long as needed for internal reporting and are overwritten by each refresh. Access to QuickBooks Online may be revoked at any time by our administrators via Intuit's connected applications settings, which immediately ends the Application's ability to retrieve data. Contact Questions about this policy may be directed to American Patio Covers through our primary business contact channels.
